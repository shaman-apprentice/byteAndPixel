You control a few monsters on a quest to clean this world of spiders and evil fog.
Select a monster with left-click. A selected Monster carries out orders with right-click.
A monster can do a limited number of actions in a turn. If you have depleted the action-points of all of your monsters press the "End Turn"-Button.
When you ended your turn the spiders will move to attack you and the spider dens will spawn new spiders.

Hotkeys:
WEADYX - will move your Monster one step
M - select move action
T - cleanse a tile of fog
1234567890 - select an attack
space - to rest and recover energy
